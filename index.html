My Projects :

Akan names
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their
first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Most people
do know when their birthdays are, but some might not know what day of the week they were born.
Akan Name Application helps users get their name by providing their gender and date of birth.
